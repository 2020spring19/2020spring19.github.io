Components of Physical Fitness Jigsaw Activity

In this activity you will work in a group of five students. Each member of the group will be given one component of physical fitness. You will research this component and become an "expert" on it. After researching, you will present your reserch to your group and teach them about your component of physical fitness.

Directions:

  1. Get together with your pre-assigned group of five students.
  2. Each member of the group will be assigned a number from 1 to 5. Look at the table below to see what component of physical fitness corresponds with your number.
  3. Use the 3 links provided to study your topic and become an "expert" on it.
  4. Join a new group with students that have the same component of physical fitness as you. Discuss your findings on this component and answer any questions anyone might have.
  5. Finally, return to your original group. Each student will get a chance to teach the group about the component of research that they researched.
  6. Once all groups are finished, the class will have a quiz on all five components of physical fitness.
