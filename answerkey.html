Plagiarism and the Internet Scavenger Hunt - Answer Key

How has checking for plagiarism become easier?

Checking for plagiarism has become easier due to an online plagiarism checker, which scans thousands of sites with hundreds of words on each, thoroughly checking for copied work.

How has technology made it easier to plagiarize?

Technology has made it easier to plagiarize because there is an incredible volume of information at our fingertips, and it is extremely easy to copy and paste this information without paraphrasing and/or citing the source.

How does social media play a big role in plagiarism?

Social media plays a big role in plagiarism because there is lots of different content being posted each day by people who might not realize that they’re plagiarizing by copying someone else’s post.
