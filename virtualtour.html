Today you will take a virtual tour to the bottom of the ocean! The ocean has 5 main layers: the epipelagic zone, the mesopelagic zone, the bathypelagic zone, the abyssopelagic zone, and the hadalpelagic zone. During the tour you will learn about the characteristics of each layer, as well as what forms of life live in each layer.

Use the navigation bar above to visit and learn about each layer of the ocean. After touring all 5 layers, take the quiz at the end of the tour.
